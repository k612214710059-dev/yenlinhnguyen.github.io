Factors affecting Gen Z's participation in the night-time economy in Hanoi
Nguyen Thi Yen Linh, Nguyen Phuong Thao, Phan Phuong Anh, Doan Thi Nguyet Anh, Tu Hong Nhung
Vietnam Trade and Industry Review
PDF / Full Paper

This study investigates the factors influencing Gen Z’s participation in the night-time economy in Hanoi across three roles: consumer, employee, and entrepreneur. The study offers recommendations for policymakers to effectively manage and enhance the participation of Gen Z in Hanoi's night-time economy, fostering a vibrant and inclusive environment that supports both individual growth and broader economic development. The research successfully received research funding from Foreign Trade University.
